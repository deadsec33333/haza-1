Beloved Koh Rong Monkey “Lanu” Faces Heartbreaking Fate After Tourist Incident

Business

Publication date
17 February 2024 | 16:10 ICT

Reporter : Sponsored

More Topic

KOH RONG, CAMBODIA — The island of Koh Rong has been struck by heartbreak and disbelief as news spreads of the government’s decision to euthanize Lanu, a beloved monkey who has been a local legend for years. Known for his playful antics and gentle interactions with villagers and visitors alike, Lanu was cherished by the island’s residents and often seen as a symbol of the island’s vibrant wildlife.

Lanu, a long-tailed macaque, had become a fixture on the island, often spotted swinging through trees near the beach or playfully interacting with locals who offered him fruit and affection. Koh Rong’s residents say he was more than just a monkey—he was part of their community. “We all loved him. He was like one of us,” said local resident Chea, wiping away tears. “Lanu brought us joy every day. He was family.”

However, the beloved macaque’s fate took a turn recently when a backpacker unknowingly got too close to Lanu, startling the animal and leading to a bite. While minor, the incident prompted health officials to step in, citing concerns about potential risks to public safety and the necessity to prevent further incidents.

Despite local outcry and pleas from island residents who insisted that the incident was an isolated one, the government announced its decision to euthanize Lanu. “We understand the importance of preserving wildlife and acknowledge Lanu’s special place in the hearts of Koh Rong’s people,” a government spokesperson stated, “but we must prioritize public safety.”

The news has left many on the island devastated, with a community petition gathering signatures from locals and tourists alike to spare Lanu’s life. “It’s hard to imagine the island without him,” said Dara, a fisherman who often shared his catch with Lanu. “He was a legend here. Koh Rong won’t be the same.”

As the island comes to terms with the impending loss, locals are working to honor Lanu’s legacy, hoping to preserve his memory in a way that celebrates the joy he brought to Koh Rong.
